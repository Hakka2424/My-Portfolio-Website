Sinister Kicks

Sinister Kicks is a brand I created to represent my journey in the sneaker world. From buying and selling locally to expanding into global marketplaces, I built this business with a passion for sneakers and the culture around them.

The Sinister Kicks logo is a custom design I made myself, inspired by the Sinister Six from Marvel. I chose Mysterio as the main figure because his illusions reflect how unpredictable the sneaker market can be. Prices go up and down, hype shifts, and only those who understand the game can stay ahead.

The sneaker in the logo is the Jordan 1 Union Black Toe, one of the most popular shoes in sneaker culture. I picked this sneaker because it is my most expensive shoe in my collection and it represents everything about my business: exclusivity, premium quality, and high demand. Just like this shoe, I focus on securing rare and limited edition releases that are tough to find but highly valued by collectors.
